What is AK HOUSEMovement?

The AK HOUSE Movement is collective of both Local, National and International talents who are striving to put Alaska on the map as a home for House Music.

The movement was founded by Chris Sanchez and his wife Rosa in 2012. Alaska is just the beginning, in the crusade to put House back into the minds and souls of those who may and may not know where their roots lie. AKHM is a welcoming family of like minded house heads that aims to sow new seeds in the growing house community. We seek to help to educate on the founding bases that makes up everything House music related today.
